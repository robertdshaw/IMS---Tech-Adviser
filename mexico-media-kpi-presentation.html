Mexico Media Infrastructure Assessment

Measuring Democratic Resilience Through Data-Driven KPIs

PROTOTYPE DEMONSTRATION • 2025

The Challenge We're Solving

Mexico's Media Reality

  • High concentration of media ownership limiting diverse perspectives
  • Journalists face digital surveillance and harassment
  • Communities lack voice in media coverage decisions
  • Disinformation spikes during critical periods (elections)

Current Blind Spots

Organizations supporting Mexican media lack real-time, quantifiable metrics to measure the effectiveness of their interventions in strengthening democratic media infrastructure.

The Gap

No existing framework measures media infrastructure health across information integrity, user rights, and community control simultaneously.

Our Solution: KPI Framework

📊

Information Integrity

Can people distinguish truth from disinformation? Measures correction speed, source diversity, fact-checking effectiveness.

🛡️

User Rights Protection

Are journalists safe from surveillance? Measures encryption adoption, incident response, security training.

👥

Community Control

Do communities influence coverage? Measures editorial participation, revenue transparency, feedback mechanisms.

Unified Scoring System

These three dimensions combine into a single Mexico Infrastructure Score and Journalist Safety Index, providing clear, actionable metrics for funders and implementers.
